To create a free email, visit a provider like Gmail, Outlook, or Yahoo and click "Sign up" or "Create account". You will be prompted to enter your personal information, choose a username and password, and agree to the terms of service. Some providers may require a phone number for verification, which can help secure your account. 
For Gmail
Go to the Google Account sign-in page and click "Create account".
Select "For my personal use".
Enter your first and last name.
Choose a username (the part before @gmail.com) and create a password that is at least 8 characters long and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
Click "Next" and provide additional information like your birthdate and gender if prompted.
Review the terms and conditions and click "I Agree" to create your account. 
For Outlook.com
Go to the Outlook.com website and click "Create free account".
Choose to get a new email address ending in either "@outlook.com" or "@hotmail.com".
Enter your desired username and a strong password.
Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the sign-up process.
